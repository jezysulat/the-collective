The Origin Story

How The Collective
Was Born

— Alec Cuenca, Take Off Summit

On a call shortly after, Kerby suggested a small but meaningful tweak: "We're one content away to change one's life." One word changed — and the whole mission shifted from personal gain to collective impact.

During that same call, Kerby nudged Jonathan to start posting. Jez jumped in and became the first to actually post — lighting the spark for everyone else. What started as a simple nudge became something far bigger than any of them originally planned.

Not from a polished business plan. From a real moment of conviction shared between founders who refused to stay invisible.
